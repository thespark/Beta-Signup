What We Do

THE SPARK, Inc. ("The Spark") provides services through its websites, programs and computer servers, including but not limited to a forum to facilitate activism through the sharing of information and ideas online, in order to advance public interests. This is accomplished by enabling users to post and vote on "problems" that they would like the community to solve. Users then post and vote on "solutions" to those problems, so that the community can take action in order to catalyze human advancement.

"Problem" refers to all new threats, discussions, entries generated by the public about the concerns existing in the community.

"Solution" refers to responses to "problems" generated by the public with a purpose to solve, suggest and propose an action plan that will be valid, proper and a legal solution to the problem.

"Action" refers to specific activities that users can participate in, in order to implement the "solution" to solve the "problem."
