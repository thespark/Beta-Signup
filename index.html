The most popular problems are voted to the top. Users then post and vote on SOLUTIONS so that the community can take ACTION. Leading Solution

Sparked by Anne Smith on 5/16/12 at 11:34pm

Let's send a clear message to the CEOs of Smartphone manufacturers: we demand ethical treatment of all employees in their supply chain, including employees of manufacturing partners. We want to know how and when they will mandate fair treatment, and we will start by publicly asking the CEO of company X to commit to a specific plan/timeframe. Let's get one million people to view this issue to ensure that we get an adequate response!

Actions

  • Sign this open letter to the CEO by November 15th
  • Help us achieve one million participants by tweeting and posting this Spark
  • Check back here on December 15th (the CEOs deadline to respond) to see if this plan is adequate, or if we need to take additional action
